About Us

Mom's Kitchen

Mom's Kitchen is an online and mobile food ordering system which we have developed for tiffin centers and housewife. Through Mom's Kitchen we are helping customers to discover the best tiffin centers in city. If you are tiffin centers owner and housewife, you can easily register to our app and upload menu to start receiving online orders through Mom's Kitchen

Mission

Our Mission is to enables anyone to have anything(basically food) delivered on demand

Vision

Mom's kitchen teams are working to establish a social impact by creating employment for both unemployed male and female who are kin to be self-employed.
